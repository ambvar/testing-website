• How To
  • Login
  • Make sure you know your ID number. You will be loging in with that.
  • You will have to request your password from your professor, they will create your password for you.
  • Once you have your ID and password, you can now login to the system.
  • After logging in, you will see all exams/tests/quizzes to take.
  • Select one and then hit submit to begin. Once you hit submit, you will not be able to go back.
  • Once you finish answering all the questions, hit submit.
  • On the next page, you will see your results. Each question is worth a certain amount of points and there is no partial credit.
